Hi there!

My name is Justin West and I am a Web Developer. I discoverd my love for design and all things tech in Venture Camps at McMaster University between the ages of 9 and 13. Subsequently I made simple websites, logos, animations and music videos. By 19 I had my own site for my music. After a few years of composing music for video games and trailers I decided to pursue an education in digital media. I am now in my last semester in Fanshawe's Interactive Media Design program and I am expected to graduate by April 2017.
