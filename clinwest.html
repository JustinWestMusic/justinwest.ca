CLINWest Research Inc.

Company Site

Project Overview

For this freelance project I was asked by my Dad to bring his old site into 2017. I had to completely re-design and re-organize all the content from his previous site into the new one.

I started with his logo which was in dire need of some modern touch ups. Once the colour scheme was set in stone I was able to come up with the layout for his new site. Finally after adding new images, responsivness and new custom functionalities the site was ready to replace his old one.

I designed the site from scratch using Foundation as my front-end framework. The contact form was also coded from the ground up and is fully functional.

Technologies

  • CSS3
  • PHP
  • Foundation Framework
  • Fully Responsive
