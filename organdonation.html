Organ Donation

Client Site

Project Overview

For this client project I worked with Meredith House, Suada Gagulic and Mathew Russello to create a marketing campgaign for Organ Donation in Ontario. My role was to help code the front-end of the site as well as building a custom Content Management System for easy updating of the main elements on the site.

Our tagline was Life Is Worth Sharing which we thought was a very strong and emotive slogan to promote organ donation in Ontario.
