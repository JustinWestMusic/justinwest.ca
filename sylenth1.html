Sylenth1

Product Demo

Project Overview

For this assignment I was asked to create a product demo showcasing a product of my choice. I chose LennarDigital's flagship synthesizer Sylenth1. Their current site lacked in design aesthetics and I therefore decided to completely overhaul the look and feel of the site using crisp colors/gradients, images and fluid animations.

I designed the site from scratch using Foundation as my front-end framework. The site colours were sampled directly from the synthesizer itself to create a better cohesion between the various images of the product and the coloured section backgrounds.

Technologies

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Greensock Animation
  • Foundation Framework
  • Fully Responsive
